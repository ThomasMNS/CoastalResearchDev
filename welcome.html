Welcome!

Posted by Thomas Burke on Tue 14 March 2017

Hello, and welcome. This website is intented to provide information on the research being carried out at Lancaster University, relating to the interactions between water, sediment and artificial structures in coastal and riverine environments.

Along with showcasing some of our more recent research, the site also contains information on some of the tools we have developed that are freely available for your own use.

If you would like to learn more about us, or get in touch, please visit the People page..

Thank you for visiting.
