People

People currently involved in coastal research activities at Lancaster include:

Dr Suzana Ilic

Senior Lecturer, Lancaster Environment Centre

Links: University Profile, Twitter

Suzana Ilic is a Senior Lecturer in the Lancaster Environment Centre. Her research interest is in understanding coastal hydrodynamics, sediment transport and the resulting morphological changes; and in developing tools for coastal managers and consultants that can be used to predict of future changes.

Dr Michael James

Senior Lecturer, Lancaster Environment Centre

Links: University Profile, Homepage

Michael James is a Senior Lecturer in the Lancaster Environment Centre. His research involves understanding Earth surface change processes, using field measurements of active processes using laser scanning, close-range photogrammetry, mm-wave radar, infrared and remote time-lapse imaging, and UAVs.

Laura O'Keefe

Innovation Fellow

Links: University Profile

Laura O'Keefe is a PhD student in the Lancaster Environment Centre. Her research has involved investigating the impacts of small scale low head hydropower on the aquatic environment.

Thomas Burke

Intern

Links: Homepage

Thomas Burke is a former MSci student and current intern at the Lancaster Environment Centre, interested in using GIS and remote sensing to investigate the environment.
