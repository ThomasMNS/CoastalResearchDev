Welcome

We are a group of academics researching the interactions between water, sediment and artificial structures in coastal and riverine environments. We aim to further knowledge in areas such as flooding, erosion and renewable energy generation, and are especially known for our successful collaborations with partners across academia, business and government.

Wide-ranging expertise

Our experience includes GIS, remote sensing, numerical modelling, laboratory modelling and long and short term field campaigns across a variety of sites in the U.K and abroad.

Actionable research

Our research is regularly practical and applied in nature, and tackles the important issues facing the world today including coastal erosion and renewable energy generation, furthering knowledge and helping to inform decision makers.
